EXHIBIT A
                                FEE SCHEDULE
I. There is no set-up cost or fixed monthly cost
II. Delivery or Takeout
For delivery or takeout orders placed on the Dining Portal or on Your Website, We will charge You a flat fee of 8.5% of the amount of the total final order placed by the user (EXCLUDING any delivery fee, tax, and tip). Other than the ACH fee described in the agreement, We will not charge any additional credit card processing or bank processing fees for the transaction.
III. Delivery by Munch Ado
For delivery orders placed on the Dining Portal or on Your Website that will be delivered by Munch Ado through a delivery logistics partner, We will charge You a flat fee of 17.5% of the amount of the total final order placed by the user (EXCLUDING any delivery fee, tax, and tip). We will deliver orders to a 1 mile radius. The fee for any delivery beyond 1 mile will be agreed between You and Us. Other than the ACH fee described in the agreement, We will not charge any additional credit card processing or bank processing fees for the transaction.
IV. Reservations
When users make reservations for dining at your restaurant, We will charge You at the rate of $1 (One US Dollar) per seat reserved. If a user, after making reservations, does not show up, You must inform us on the same day as the reservation by emailing us at wecare@munchado.com and We will not charge You. We will invoice You once a month for the reservations made during a one month period for your restaurant from the Dining Portal. As part of an introductory promotion, We are waiving the $1 per seat charge on reservations for the duration of this contract.
V. Prepaid Reservations
When users make a prepaid reservation at your restaurant, We will charge You a flat fee of 8.5% of the amount of the total prepaid order placed by the user (including beverages, but EXCLUDING tax, and tip)
VI. Coupons
For coupons, We will charge You a flat fee of 10% of the cash value of the coupon each time it is used by a customer.
VII. Payment Terms
For delivery and takeout, We will collect payment from users and forward these amounts to You less the fee as described in Section II above on a weekly basis. For reservations and coupons, We will invoice You once a week.
